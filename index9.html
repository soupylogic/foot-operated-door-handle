🚪 Smart Hygiene Door

Advanced Mechanism Prototype

Problem Statement

People face critical hygiene risks when opening doors in public places. Direct hand contact spreads germs, bacteria, and viruses—a major health concern.

Our Solution

A completely hands-free, foot-operated door handle mechanism using an infrared sensor, MCU, and motorized actuator to physically pull the handle down for you.
